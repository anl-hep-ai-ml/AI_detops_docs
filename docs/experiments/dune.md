# DUNE

The Deep Underground Neutrino Experiment (DUNE) at Fermilab and the Sanford Underground Research Facility (SURF) studies neutrino oscillations and searches for proton decay using liquid argon time projection chambers (LArTPCs). Argonne National Laboratory is a major contributor to the DUNE far detector construction effort.

## Application

Anomaly detection is applied to waveform data from the **Photon Detection System (PDS)** of the Vertical Drift prototype detector (PD-VD). The PDS records scintillation light across 19 channels at 1024 time ticks per event. AD targets readout faults and unusual light yield patterns that may indicate detector or electronics issues.
