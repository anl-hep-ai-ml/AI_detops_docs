# CMS

The CMS (Compact Muon Solenoid) experiment at CERN's Large Hadron Collider is the second of the two general-purpose detectors at the LHC, recording proton-proton collisions at up to √s = 13.6 TeV across O(100M) readout channels.

## Application

Anomaly detection is applied to time-series monitoring quantities from the CMS detector readout and data-quality monitoring systems. Operating at this level preserves sensitivity to hardware faults and unusual physics signatures before information is lost in the offline reconstruction chain.
