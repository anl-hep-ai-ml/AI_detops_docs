# ATLAS

The ATLAS experiment at CERN's Large Hadron Collider is one of the two general-purpose detectors at the LHC, recording proton-proton collisions at up to √s = 13.6 TeV across O(100M) readout channels.

## Application

Anomaly detection is applied to time-series output from the **High-Level Trigger (HLT)** — rate and quality monitoring quantities produced online during data-taking. Operating at this level preserves sensitivity to hardware faults and unusual physics signatures before any information is lost in the offline reconstruction chain.
