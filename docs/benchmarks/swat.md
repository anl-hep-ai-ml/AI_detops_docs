# SWAT — Secure Water Treatment

SWAT is a dataset collected from a real operational water treatment testbed (SWaT) at the iTrust research centre, Singapore. The dataset captures 51 sensor and actuator channels over 11 days of normal operation followed by a period in which 36 physical attacks were launched on the system. It is a standard benchmark for anomaly detection in industrial control systems (ICS).

## Dataset Statistics

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Channels | 51 |
| Normal duration | 7 days |
| Attack duration | 4 days |
| Number of attacks | 36 |
| Anomaly rate | ~12% |
